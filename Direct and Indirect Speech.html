Direct and Indirect Speech

Definition:
- Direct Speech - When the reporter quotes the actual words of the speaker. It is always written in inverted commas. (‡§ï‡§ø‡§∏‡•Ä ‡§µ‡§ï‡•ç‡§§‡§æ ‡§¶‡•ç‡§µ‡§æ‡§∞‡§æ ‡§ï‡§π‡•á ‡§π‡•Å‡§è ‡§∂‡§¨‡•ç‡§¶‡•ã‡§Ç ‡§ï‡•ã ‡§¨‡§ø‡§®‡§æ ‡§™‡§∞‡§ø‡§µ‡§∞‡•ç‡§§‡§® ‡§ú‡•ç‡§Ø‡•ã‡§Ç ‡§ï‡§æ ‡§§‡•ç‡§Ø‡•ã ‡§≤‡§ø‡§ñ ‡§¶‡•á‡§Ç, ‡§§‡•ã ‡§â‡§∏‡•á Direct speech ‡§ï‡§π‡§§‡•á ‡§π‡•à‡•§)

- Indirect Speech - When the reporter does not reproduce the actual words of the speaker but give the meaning of his own words, inverted commas are not used. (‡§ï‡§ø‡§∏‡•Ä ‡§µ‡§ï‡•ç‡§§‡§æ ‡§ï‡•á ‡§ï‡§π‡•á ‡§π‡•Å‡§è ‡§∂‡§¨‡•ç‡§¶‡•ã‡§Ç ‡§Æ‡•á‡§Ç ‡§ï‡§∞‡•ç‡§§‡•ç‡§§‡§æ ‡§ï‡•ç‡§∞‡§ø‡§Ø‡§æ ‡§Æ‡•á‡§Ç ‡§™‡§∞‡§ø‡§µ‡§∞‡•ç‡§§‡§® ‡§ï‡§∞‡§ï‡•á ‡§≤‡§ø‡§ñ‡§æ ‡§ú‡§æ‡§§‡§æ ‡§π‡•à, ‡§§‡•ã ‡§â‡§∏‡•á Indirect speech ‡§ï‡§π‡§§‡•á ‡§π‡•à‡•§)
Examples:
• Indirect: Ram said that he was very busy then.
Indirect: Rani told me that I was a liar.
•In the above sentences we changed pronouns, verb and adverb and also added a connective (that). In few sentences reported verb is also changed.

‚ùÉ Rules For Changing Direct into Indirect
• Reporting verb is changed according to the form and sense of the sentence.
• Inverted commas are removed in the indirect-speech.
• Connective word is used in the beginning of the reported speech.
• Verb of the reported speech is changed according to the form and sense of the sentence.
• Persons of the reported speech are changed.
• Words showing nearness, time or place are changed.
1. Change in Pronouns:
- The first person of Reported Speech is changed as per the subject of Reporting Verb.
- The second person of Reported Speech is changed as per the object of Reporting Verb.
- The third person of Reported Speech is not changed.
